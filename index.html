About Me

I'm Apoorv, a business analyst with a strong background in data analytics and a fervent commitment to leveraging insights for informed decision-making. I specialize in a comprehensive range of services encompassing data collection, preparation, analysis, and management, ensuring impactful presentations of insights.

Having spent over three years at Amazon as a Quality Analyst, I honed my skills in data, audits, and policy making. Now, as a Business Analyst at Sanofi, I focus on commercial analytics for the US market, primarily for the Tzield and Endocrine brands. My belief in the transformative power of technology-driven solutions aligns with my goal to contribute to impactful programs on a large scale and democratize access to technology.

With a proficiency in statistical and algebraic techniques, coupled with my certification as a programmer, I have successfully developed and deployed machine learning models on servers. My experience extends to collaborating with both technical and non-technical teams, emphasizing the importance of making data accessible and useful for all stakeholders.

If you believe that my skills and experience could be of value to your organization, please feel free to reach out to me by email. I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate and bring my passion for data to your organization.
