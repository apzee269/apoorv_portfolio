About Me

I am Apoorv, a data and quality analyst with a passion for helping organizations make the most of their data. I offer a comprehensive range of services, from data collection and preparation, to analysis and management, to impactful presentation of insights. My goal is to support organizations in making evidence-driven decisions and sharing their insights with new audiences.

I bring a strong background in data gathering and preparation, as well as advanced analytical skills, including proficiency in statistical and algebraic techniques. As a certified programmer, I am able to create machine learning models that can be deployed on a server. I have experience working with both technical and non-technical personnel, and I am committed to making data accessible and useful for all.

I am currently employed at Amazon as a Quality Analyst, where I have over 3 years of experience working with data, audits, and policy making. I genuinely believe in the power of technology-driven solutions to deliver public services that are in tune with the aspirations of billions of people and to democratize access to technology. I have a deep understanding of data science and its potential to support impactful programs at scale.

If you believe that my skills and experience could be of value to your organization, please feel free to reach out to me by email. I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate and bring my passion for data to your organization.
