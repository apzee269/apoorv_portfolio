About Me

I am Apoorv, a data and quality analyst, passionate about working with organisations that want to make effective use of their data; from the first ideas around what you want to measure and how to capture data accurately, through data preparation, analysis and management, all the way to impactful presentation of insights.
I can support each step of the process to enable your organisation in making evidence-driven decisions or share insights with new audiences. I am familiar with gathering, cleaning and organizing data for use by technical and non-technical personnel, creating machine learning models using Advanced understanding of statistical, algebraic and other analytical techniques and deploying them on the server.

Currently I am working at Amazon as a Quality Analyst, with 3+ years of experience cutting across data, audits, and policy making. I genuinely believe that open, inclusive and scalable technological interventions through digital public goods can effectively deliver public services that are in tune with the aspirations of billions of people while democratising access to technology for all. I have the understanding of data science and technology-driven sustainable solutions for implementing impactful programmes at scale. Along with this, I have worked on multiple data science projects and deployed them on server to make a full-fledged model. I identify as a self-taught programmer. I am passionate about leveraging data science to help inform meaningful policies that advance the company’s growth

In my free time I love reading books, cooking, solving rubiks cube and on weekends, I prefer going on a long drive.
