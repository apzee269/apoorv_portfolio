About Me

I am a Senior Analyst in Pharma Analytics and Business Intelligence, with experience turning complex healthcare data into insights that drive decisions. My work spans Reporting, Patient Services (PSS), Omnichannel data engineering, and Advanced Analytics enablement.

At Sanofi, I specialize in building interactive dashboards and data pipelines using Power BI, Python, SQL, and Snowflake. I designed the Unified Tzield Dashboard, which brings together patient journey data (screening, enrollment, infusion, and revenue) with customer-facing activities such as KAM calls and lunch & learns. This solution was adopted by brand, field, and market teams and received strong feedback for its impact. I also developed a Global Expense Tracker to monitor multi-million-dollar budgets and an Employee Bandwidth Tool that improved adherence from 65% to 90% with 100% adoption across GTMC.

Beyond reporting, I have experience consolidating omnichannel datasets from Veeva CRM, Merkle, Google Analytics, and call center data. I also collaborated with the Advanced Analytics team to translate forecasting and machine learning outputs into business-facing dashboards that support supply and budget planning.

Recognized with six corporate awards in two years, I also mentor junior analysts, contribute to governance routines, and serve as an interview panel member, helping shape the team’s growth.
